TeenSafe&#8217;s full suite of monitoring services including viewing your teen&#8217;s text/SMS/iMessages and deleted messages, call logs and contacts, WhatsApp and Kik messages and location tracking are all available at <a class="text-decoration-underline" style="color: #0077ee !important;" href="https://www.teensafe.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.TeenSafe.com</a>.<a href="https://www.teensafe.com/monitor/"> Learn More &gt;&gt;</a></div>
<div class="text-wrap"><b>TeenSafe Control</b><br />
TeenSafe Control is an App for iOS and Android that enables parents to block apps, set restrictions, or pause the teen&#8217;s phone at bedtime, dinnertime, study time, and to prevent texting while driving, among many other uses. <a href="https://www.teensafe.com/control/">Learn More &gt;&gt;</a></div>
</div>
</li>
<li class=""><a class="opener" href="#">How does TeenSafe Control work?</a>
<div class="slide" style="display: block; position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap"><strong>TeenSafe Control</strong> uses Mobile Device Management (MDM) Protocol, similar to the way corporations secure devices used on their networks in order to protect their network from Internet threats. An MDM certificate is installed on your teen’s iPhone and paired with the TeenSafe Control App on your iPhone, giving you the power to control it remotely. TeenSafe Control’s proprietary method of incorporating MDM makes it difficult for your teenager to disable or remove it. In cases where they find a way, we will alert you via email.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class=""><a class="opener" href="#">What exactly can I do with TeenSafe Control?</a>
<div class="slide" style="display: block; position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">
<p><strong>TeenSafe Control</strong> enables parents to remotely control their teen’s phone. Here are some examples of how parents find it useful:</p>
<ul>
<li>Pause your teen&#8217;s phone during dinner, bedtime and to stop texting while driving</li>
<li>Block apps like SnapChat, in-app purchases and access to the app store</li>
<li>Schedule restrictions on phone and app usage around your family&#8217;s routine (homework, dinner, bedtime)- <em>coming in 2017</em></li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</li>
<li class=""><a class="opener" href="#">How do I cancel my TeenSafe account?</a>
<div class="slide" style="display: block; position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">
<p>If you wish to cancel your TeenSafe account, please contact support at 1-800-245-8598.</p>
<p>Please note: we <b>cannot</b> accept cancellation requests over chat or email as we must confirm we are speaking with the parent in charge of the account.
</div>
</div>
</li>
<li><a class="opener" href="#">What does TeenSafe enable me to see? </a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">
<p>Using TeenSafe, parents can see:</p>
<ul class="accordion-ul">
<li>Sent, received and deleted SMS and iMessages.</li>
<li>Call logs of sent and received phone calls.</li>
<li>Device location and location history.</li>
<li>Web browsing history, bookmarks and contacts.</li>
<li>Messages sent via Whatsapp and Kik Messenger.</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
<ul class="accordion">
<li><a class="opener" href="#">Does my teen need to know that I’m using TeenSafe?</a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">Every parent&#8217;s situation is unique and only a parent can decide whether to inform their teen of their intent to use the <a class="text-decoration-underline" style="color: #0077ee !important;" href="https://www.teensafe.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">www.TeenSafe.com</a> monitoring service and TeenSafe Monitor. Many parents choose to tell their teen that they&#8217;re using TeenSafe, and disclosure to teens is recommended by TeenSafe. In California, or where the interpretation of privacy laws with respect to parental monitoring is not settled, TeenSafe is exploring ways in which the monitoring service can provide notice to teens.<br />
When using <strong>TeenSafe Control</strong>, your teen’s phone will state “This phone is being managed by TeenSafe.”</div>
</div>
</li>
<li><a class="opener" href="#">Does TeenSafe work with all mobile phones? </a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">
<p>TeenSafe’s monitoring service works with iPhone and Android smartphones. It also works with iPads, the iPod touch and Android pre-paid phones. TeenSafe does not currently work with Blackberries or Windows phones.</p>
<p>TeenSafe Control currently works when both parent and child have iPhones. An Android version is due in 2017.</p>
</div>
</div>
</li>
<li><a class="opener" href="#">Will I be able to see images or video sent via text? </a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">No. It is TeenSafe’s policy to not display images nor videos sent via text from their phone.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li><a class="opener" href="#">How is TeenSafe different?</a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">Your <a href="//www.teensafe.com">www.TeenSafe.com</a> login works without requiring your child’s iPhone to be “jailbroken” nor Android phones to be “Rooted”. TeenSafe does not alter an iPhone in anyway and does not violate the phone’s warranty. TeenSafe offers parents a single and secure method by which they can access and monitor their teen’s digital lives.</div>
</div>
</li>
<li><a class="opener" href="#">Can I add more than one teen?</a>
<div class="slide" style="position: absolute; top: -9999px; left: -9999px; width: 525px;">
<div class="text-wrap">Yes. You can add an unlimited number of teens for no additional cost.</div>
