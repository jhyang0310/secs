		<h2>Info</h2>
		
		<div class="content">
		<p><strong>Gold Films Ltd</strong> was created in 2008 by <strong>Martin Owen</strong> and <strong>Giles Alderson</strong> to produce exciting new <strong>Film</strong> projects. Between them, they have over 20 years experience in the industry.</p>
		<p>Recently they have produced, directed and written five award winning short films including the first 3D drama to be shot in the UK.</p>
		<p>Surrounding themselves with a team of accomplished individuals, <strong>Gold Films</strong> are currently focusing on groundbreaking <strong>3D filmmaking</strong> to produce work of cutting edge calibre.</p>
		<p>After the success of their short films the pair will be directing the action thriller <strong>FOR HIRE</strong> later this year.</p>
		<p>They have also brought their team of top heads of department together to launch their 3D feature film <strong>TRANSMISSION</strong> starring Willem Dafoe and Bob Hoskins.</p>
