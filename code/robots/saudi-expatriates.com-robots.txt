<span style="font-size: x-large;"><b>SAUDI BANKS ARE GETTING READY TO PROVIDE CAR LOANS FOR WOMEN</b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: center;">
<span style="font-size: x-large;"><b><br />
</b></span></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<span style="font-size: large;">After a royal decree of King Salman on "Allowing <a href="http://www.saudi-expatriates.com/2017/09/grant-driving-license-for-women-ordered.html" target="_blank">Women to drive in Saudi Arabia</a>", Now Banks are getting ready to provide car loans for women as there will be a huge demand in car sales.&nbsp;</span></div>
